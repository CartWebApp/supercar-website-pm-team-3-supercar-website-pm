Black Widow

This high-performance vehicle showcases Trion's commitment to cutting-edge technology, luxury, and sustainability, pushing over 1400 horsepower with advanced axial flux electric motors. The Black Widow combines sleek aerodynamic design with an all-electric powertrain, reflecting Trion's vision of where high-performance meets environmental responsibility. More than just a supercar, it represents the company's bold ambition to lead the next generation of electric vehicles, blending power, precision, and eco-conscious engineering to set a new standard in luxury performance.
